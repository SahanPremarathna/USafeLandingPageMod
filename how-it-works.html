Core Capabilities

The main feature panel behind the USafe experience.

These capabilities are designed to interlock. Each one can help on its own, but together they turn navigation into a proactive safety product.

Each feature will also have its own dedicated sub-page.

Real-Time Safety Score

Continuous route scoring turns complex city signals into a simple confidence readout the user can understand at a glance.

Risk Heatmap

Dynamic hotspots show where caution is rising, helping users understand the shape of the environment around them.

Smart Safe Navigation

USafe suggests safer route options, not just faster ones, balancing movement, awareness, and risk reduction.

Emergency SOS

One action can trigger alerts, location sharing, and coordinated support when speed matters most.

Silent Emergency Call

When speaking is unsafe, discreet escalation tools preserve the chance to act without drawing attention.

SafePath Guardian

Guardian mode turns trusted people into a coordinated safety network.

SafePath Guardian gives selected contacts a secure browser-based dashboard with live journey visibility, checkpoint progress, and action-ready emergency status.

  • Trusted contacts join through a secure live link
  • Browser dashboard shows route, status, and last known context
  • Checkpoint updates confirm progress without friction
  • Instant action protocol activates when the journey goes off track
